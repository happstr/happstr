  • Tap-in to global happiness.

    Happstr is about challenging yourself to tap-in every time you are happy, and share that happiness with your friends. Obviously, you're happier when your friends are happy (by about 15%), but research shows that you're also 10% happier when their friends are happy. Even crazier, a friend of a friend of a friend being happy increases your happiness by about 6%. We aim to amplify world happiness by leveraging this network effect.

    Feeling pretty good? Let others know.

    Happstr starts with a simple web app for you to keep track of your happiness. When you tap the happy button your elation gets tagged to a global map. Then discover the happiness around you and explore happy tap-ins around the world.
